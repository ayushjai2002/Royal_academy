Pride In Excellence

Delhi Public School, Chhatarpur (Madhya Pradesh) aims to deliver balanced educational and personality skills within a safe, stimulating environment for each child to grow to the greatest potential.

Delhi Public School, Chhatarpur is managed by Kunal Educational Society registered in New Delhi.

OUR VISION

Our vision is to ignite curious minds and be an inspirational educational institute with real responsibility for the development of society.

ADMISSIONS OPEN

Choosing the right school to nurture young minds can be a crucial decision. It may affect the child's social and academic performance in the future!

The school offers an amiable, indulgent, and value-based learning experience with an equal emphasis on life skills, attitudes, values, and achievement in outdoor co-curricular activities encompassing sports and games.
