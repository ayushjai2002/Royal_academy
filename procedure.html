ADMISSION PROCEDURE

THE SIMPLEST WAY IS...
  • Click here to call our admission counsellor.
  • The admission counsellor will contact you during school hrs. to schedule, the Interaction/Assessment round as per the class applied for.
  • Upon Selection, submit the required documents and the fee.
Required Documents
  • Aadhaar Card of the student.
  • Aadhaar Card of the parents.
  • Birth Certificate.
  • Medical Certificate / Blood Report.
  • 3 Passport Size photographs of the student and parents.
  • Transfer Certificate. (TC if applicable)
  • Report Card of the previous year (where applicable).
